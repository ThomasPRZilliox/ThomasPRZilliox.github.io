DB Browser for SQLite

vailable on : Windows 10 and later, MAC OS.

Efficiently view your data and executes queries of your sqlite database. DB Browser for SQLite (DB4S) will also allow you test some queries as you can decide to apply or discard them to your current database.

ScreenToGif

Available on : Windows 10 and later.

Easily record your screen and save it as a gif. Really useful for presentation and ticket creation about an User Interface bug/ feature.
